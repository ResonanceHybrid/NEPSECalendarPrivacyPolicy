Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us.

Overview

NEPSE Calendar ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application that provides Nepal Stock Exchange (NEPSE) event information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

NEPSE Calendar is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Contact information (email, phone numbers)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Financial information or investment data
  • Login credentials or authentication data
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

Firebase Remote Config

Our app uses Firebase Remote Config solely to fetch the latest NEPSE event data. This service:

  • Provides updated event information automatically
  • Does not collect any personal user data
  • Does not track individual users
  • Only delivers configuration data to the app
  • Operates anonymously without user identification

Local Data Storage

The app stores NEPSE event data locally on your device using SharedPreferences for offline functionality. This data includes only public NEPSE event information (dates, event names, types) and contains no personal information. This data is stored solely on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Internet Connection

The app requires an internet connection only to fetch the latest NEPSE event updates from Firebase Remote Config. When offline, the app functions using previously downloaded data. No personal data is transmitted during these connections.

Third-Party Services

NEPSE Calendar does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Firebase Remote Config for delivering updated event data, which operates without collecting user information.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, most data protection rights are not applicable. However, you have the right to:

  • Use the app completely anonymously
  • Clear locally stored data by uninstalling the app
  • Use the app offline without any data transmission
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
